All Things Viola

Viola Davis, current star of ABC's How To Get Away with Murder, recently won the Academy Award for her role as Rose in Fences, directed by Denzel Washington. Trained at the Julliard School, she began her career as a stage actress in New York City, garnering rave reviews for her performances in several of the plays in August Wilson's cycle. Hollywood has finally recognized her after several years in the film industry. Her fans couldn't be happier
